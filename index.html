General Notes
  • Warm up for 5–10 min before each workout (dynamic stretches, light cardio).
  • Cool down for 5–10 min after each workout (static stretches, deep breathing).
  • Listen to your body. If lower back/hip pain flares, modify or skip aggravating exercises.
  • Hydrate well and maintain a balanced diet to support fat loss and muscle gain.
